Kwik Kwiz



Each quiz contains questions from five categories: General Knowledge, Computer science, Geography, History and Animals and each question can be of easy, medium or hard difficulty. You start with a one minute timer. Giving a correct answer grants extra time, based on the difficulty of the question. On the other hand, giving a wrong answer costs you time, the amount of which also depends on the difficulty of the question. The time distribution based on question difficulty is as follows:

  • Easy questions. Correct answers grant one extra second. Wrong answers deduct three seconds.
  • Medium questions. Correct answers grant two extra seconds. Wrong answers deduct two seconds.
  • Hard questions. Correct answers grant three extra seconds. Wrong answers deduct one second.
